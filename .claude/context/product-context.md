# Product Context

## Product Definition
**GaussHub** is a comprehensive market enablement and intelligence platform specifically designed for Kunthyme's 3D Hall sensor product line. It serves as a strategic business support system that empowers technical sales teams to accelerate Design-in processes and improve customer success rates.

## Target Users & Personas

### Primary Users

#### Field Application Engineers (FAEs)
- **Role**: Front-line technical support for customers
- **Needs**: Quick access to technical solutions, efficient ticket management, design tools
- **Goals**: Reduce response times, improve solution quality, increase Design-in success
- **Pain Points**: Scattered information, repetitive questions, complex design validation

#### Sales Managers
- **Role**: Regional sales leadership and account management
- **Needs**: Pipeline visibility, team performance metrics, market intelligence
- **Goals**: Improve conversion rates, optimize resource allocation, strategic decision-making
- **Pain Points**: Lack of Design-in visibility, difficulty tracking team effectiveness

#### Product Managers
- **Role**: Product strategy and roadmap development
- **Needs**: Market feedback analysis, competitive intelligence, feature requests tracking
- **Goals**: Data-driven product decisions, customer-centric development, market positioning
- **Pain Points**: Fragmented customer feedback, limited competitive insights

### Secondary Users

#### R&D Engineers
- **Role**: Product development and technical innovation
- **Needs**: Customer feedback integration, design pattern analysis, technical requirements
- **Goals**: Customer-driven innovation, efficient development cycles
- **Usage**: Periodic access for customer insight integration

#### Executive Leadership
- **Role**: Strategic business oversight
- **Needs**: High-level KPIs, business impact metrics, strategic insights
- **Goals**: Business growth, competitive advantage, ROI optimization
- **Usage**: Dashboard access for business intelligence

## Core Use Cases

### Primary Workflows

#### Customer Support Workflow
1. **Issue Creation**: Customer submits technical inquiry or problem
2. **Intelligent Routing**: System assigns to appropriate FAE based on expertise
3. **Solution Development**: FAE uses knowledge base and tools to develop solution
4. **Customer Communication**: Structured response with technical guidance
5. **Knowledge Conversion**: Solutions converted to reusable knowledge articles

#### Design-in Process Management
1. **Opportunity Identification**: Customer expresses interest in 3D Hall sensor application
2. **Reference Design Matching**: System suggests relevant reference designs
3. **Customization Support**: FAE assists with application-specific modifications
4. **Design Validation**: Magnetic circuit analysis and validation tools
5. **Success Tracking**: Monitor progress through Design-in pipeline

#### Market Intelligence Collection
1. **Competitive Analysis**: Track competitor products and positioning
2. **Customer Feedback Aggregation**: Collect and categorize feature requests
3. **Market Trend Analysis**: Identify emerging application areas
4. **Strategic Reporting**: Generate insights for business strategy

### Secondary Use Cases

#### Knowledge Management
- Template creation and maintenance
- Best practice documentation
- Technical solution repository
- Expert knowledge capture

#### Team Collaboration
- Cross-team knowledge sharing
- Escalation management
- Peer consultation
- Training material access

## Product Requirements

### Functional Requirements

#### Core Features (V1.0 MVP)
- **Ticket Management System**: Create, assign, track, resolve customer issues
- **Knowledge Base**: Searchable repository of solutions and best practices
- **User Dashboard**: Personal workload and performance metrics
- **Basic Analytics**: Response times, resolution rates, user activity
- **Reference Design Library**: Catalog of validated sensor applications

#### Enhanced Features (V2.0)
- **AI-Powered Recommendations**: Intelligent solution suggestions
- **Magnetic Circuit Designer**: Interactive design and validation tools
- **Advanced Analytics**: Predictive insights, trend analysis, ROI tracking
- **CRM Integration**: Seamless integration with existing sales systems
- **Mobile Accessibility**: Mobile-optimized interface for field use

### Non-Functional Requirements

#### Performance
- **Response Time**: Sub-2 second page load times
- **Scalability**: Support for 500+ concurrent users
- **Availability**: 99.9% uptime SLA
- **Data Processing**: Real-time analytics and reporting

#### Security
- **Authentication**: Enterprise SSO integration
- **Authorization**: Role-based access control
- **Data Protection**: Encryption at rest and in transit
- **Audit Trail**: Complete activity logging

#### Usability
- **Interface**: Intuitive, modern web interface
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 compliance
- **Internationalization**: Multi-language support (English, Chinese)
- **Mobile**: Responsive design for mobile devices
